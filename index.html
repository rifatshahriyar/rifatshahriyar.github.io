About Me

I am working as an Associate Professor at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) of Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET). I completed a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in April 2015 from the Research School of Computer Science at Australian National University (ANU). I was a member of the Computer Systems Group, and my supervisors were Steve Blackburn and Kathryn McKinley. I completed a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in May 2007 and a Masters of Science (M.Sc.) in December 2009 from CSE, BUET.

Research Interests

My research interests are memory management specially garbage collection, programming language, high performance computing, and software engineering.
